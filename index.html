A Full CSV Editor, Viewer & Data Masking Tool — All in Your Browser

Whether you're cleaning up a spreadsheet, exploring a dataset, or preparing customer records for sharing, this tool works like a lightweight spreadsheet editor with privacy-safe masking built in. Open CSV, Excel, or JSON files, edit cells directly, sort and search your data, then selectively anonymize or redact sensitive fields before exporting.

Editing & Viewing Features

  • Multi-format import: Open .csv, .xlsx, .xls, or .json files directly — no conversion needed.
  • Inline editing: Double-click any cell to edit it, rename column headers, add or delete rows and columns.
  • Sort & search: Click a column's sort icon to order rows, or use the search box to filter across every column instantly.
  • Cell selection & copy/paste: Click and shift+click to select a range of cells, then copy or paste like a normal spreadsheet.
  • Undo/redo: Every edit, sort, and masking change can be undone with Ctrl+Z.
  • Column formatting: Mark columns as numbers or dates for cleaner display and correct sort order.
